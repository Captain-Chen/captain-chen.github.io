Who am I?

I'm Richard Nguyen, an independent developer living in Winnipeg. I work on a handful of projects that are sometimes quite useful. You can read about some of them here.

I've previously worked at KUBRA at the Missisauga location as both a client support programmer, where I maintained legacy and current applications used for billing purposes, and as a client solutions developer, where I worked on new projects with clients, designed and wrote applications to their specifications.

I raise a lot of plants, mostly the carnivorous kind, but recently started growing microgreens to eat. I do a lot of cooking and thoroughly enjoy the process from start to finish. I love reading, particularly stories with both an interesting premise and excellent world building. I also like playing the occasional video game.
